Choose the right physical format for your lifestyle.

Work Anywhere: - Freedom to move between home, office, and cafe.

Desktop PC

Power & Expandability

More Power: - Better cooling allows for higher sustained performance.
Upgradable: - Easily swap out components to extend lifespan.
Better Value: - Typically more performance per dollar.
Step 04

Pre-Purchase Checklist

Don't spend a dime until you've verified these critical points.

Generation Matters

Ensure the CPU is from the latest 2 generations (e.g., Intel 13th/14th Gen or Ryzen 7000/8000).

Expandability

Check if RAM and SSD are soldered or if there are empty slots for future upgrades.

Screen Quality

Look for IPS panels with at least 300 nits brightness and 100% sRGB coverage for good colors.

After-Sales Support

Verify that the brand has authorized service centers accessible in your region.
