Form Factor Battle

Desktop vs Laptop: Making the critical first decision.

The Laptop

Ultimate portability and convenience over raw power.

  • Mobility: Work from cafes, bed, or campus.
  • Battery Included: Built-in UPS for load shedding.
  • All-in-one: Has screen, keyboard, webcam built-in.
  • Price/Performance: More expensive for the same speed.
  • Upgrades: Often limited to RAM and Storage only.
VS

The Desktop PC

Unmatched power, cooling, and modular upgradability.

  • Raw Power: Full desktop components draw more wattage for higher FPS.
  • Longevity: You can replace individual dead parts easily.
  • Cooling: Superior airflow means longer lifespan.
  • Immobile: Stuck at your desk permanently.
  • Requires Peripherals: Need to buy Monitor, Keyboard, Mouse, UPS.

Final Pre-Purchase Checklist

Don't hand over your cash until you've verified these 4 rules.

Generation Check

CPUs age fast. Ensure you are getting Intel 12th Gen+ or Ryzen 5000+ series as a minimum. Avoid sneaky old stock.

The RAM Trap

If buying a laptop, confirm if the RAM is "Soldered" (un-upgradable) or has a "SODIMM Slot". If soldered, buy 16GB minimum.

Display Quality

Never settle for a "TN" panel in 2026. Demand an "IPS" or "OLED" panel with at least 300 nits brightness.

Authorized Warranty

Grey market units are cheaper but lack warranty. Ensure you get official warranty cards valid in Nepal.
