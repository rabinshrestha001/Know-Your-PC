Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Know Your PC ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the necessary information to ensure you have a great experience on our website. This includes:

  • Usage Data: We may collect your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you visit on our site for analytics purposes.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. (See our Cookie Policy for details).
  • Contact Information: If you use our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the email address and name you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to improve the services we provide. We use the collected data in the following ways:

  • To analyze site traffic and improve website performance and user experience.
  • To respond to your inquiries when you contact us via our contact form.

  • To show relevant advertisements and affiliate product links.

We may use third-party services that collect, monitor, and analyze data:

4. Data Protection and User Rights

We take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of your personal data. You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion or correction of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of our newsletter or marketing communications at any time.

5. We Do Not Sell Your Data

Your trust is important to us. We explicitly do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Any data shared with third parties is strictly for analytical, advertising, or operational purposes as mentioned above.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us via our Contact Us page.
