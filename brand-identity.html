Design Philosophy & Construction

Geometric Symbol Construction

The central abstract mark represents the fusion of a technical CPU/Hardware frame with a digital pulse waveform indicating "knowledge, health, and state". Built on a perfect mathematical grid, it scales flawlessly from global billboard to identical 16x16 pixel micro-favicon. The strokes align perfectly to center coordinates avoiding any awkward vector intersections, satisfying clean vector flat design and bold negative space.
